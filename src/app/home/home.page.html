Scan and Share Start Document Scan
  1. Click the "Start Document Scan" button to open the document scanner.
  2. Scan a document using your device's camera.
  3. Add as many pages as you need while scanning before hitting the next button.
  4. Once you are done scanning, click the "Next" button to proceed.
  5. You will be prompted to share with another application. Pick that application or email to attach and share your document.
